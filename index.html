About Me

Ten years as a technical writer, twenty years a philosophy student, and a lifetime as an artist have taught me attention to detail, abstract logic, and the value of experimentation and persistence. I am meticulous, perceptive and creative, skills that dovetail perfectly into web development.


For ten years, I worked as an auditor and tech writer in an environmental engineering office. I loved the purpose of the work, but after ten years in the same job I was ready for something new.


About a year ago, I needed a website for my wedding so my wife and I could coordinate everything, but I found the available wedding websites lacking. So, I decided to build my own. It was frustrating, confusing, and difficult, but ultimately it was also rewarding, and it sparked a new obsession.


I have lived in a few places, but Utah is my home. I am married, I have a daughter, and I love DIY projects. I have a degree in English and I love good writing. I'm also into snowboarding, hiking, and live music. And I love lazy Sundays, so Netflix is a dear, dear friend.
